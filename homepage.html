usourname @usourname · Jun 18

I know it's fun to share memes about senatorial candidates like Willie or Bong Revilla, ayoko rin maging KJ. However, the more we share memes about them, the more they become familiar sa masa, which increases their chances of ending up on people's ballots. If we really want to push for good governance, mas strategic if we use our platforms to promote candidates who truly deserve the spot. The more we amplify their names and platforms, the better their chances of becoming familiar to the public. Let’s face it that in the Philippines, elections are often a popularity contest. So why not help deserving candidates become more popular instead?
